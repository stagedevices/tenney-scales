### Short description (1–3 sentences)

A wide survey of European historical keyboard tunings from the medieval Pythagorean world through Renaissance/Baroque comma-management (tertial + meantone families), canonical well temperaments (key color), and 19th-century extensions, with 12-TET included as a reference endpoint. This pack includes both full chromatic temperaments and a small set of “major-scale extracts” for fast A/B auditioning inside Tenney; filenames encode family + variant so the library stays navigable. All scales are shipped as freshly-authored Scala `.scl` files suitable for CC0 release and predictable CI export into Tenney’s format.
